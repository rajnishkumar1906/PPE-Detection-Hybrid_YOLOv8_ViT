How It Works

1

Image Upload & Enhancement

Upload workplace image which is automatically enhanced using CLAHE and sharpening filters for better detection accuracy.

2

Person Detection (YOLO)

YOLOv8 model detects all persons in the image with bounding boxes and confidence scores.

3

Person Cropping

Detected persons are cropped from the original image for individual PPE analysis.

4

PPE Classification (ViT)

Vision Transformer model analyzes each person crop for PPE items: helmet, vest, gloves, and mask.

5

Result Fusion & Visualization

Results from both models are combined to generate compliance scores and visualization with bounding boxes.
